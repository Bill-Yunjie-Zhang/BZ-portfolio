Things I learnt while teaching kids:
(Order is irrelevant)

  • Always do some preparation before any class
  • Kids are kids, they need treats and punishments
  • Kids prefer playing than reviewing what they learnt in class
  • Repeating is necessary when teaching
  • Kids don't know they are learning for themselves and do cheat sometimes
  • Preference is the least wanted in classes
  • Basic stuffs like how to keep good notes should also be taught

Things I taught my students:
(Order is irrelevant)

  • Pronunciation of combinations, vowels, and combinations
  • New way of memorizing English words, instead of just rotting
  • Improve reading, listening, speaking ability
